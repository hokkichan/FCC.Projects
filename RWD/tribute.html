Steve Jobs

Co-founder, Chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc.

Picture of Steve Jobs

Here's a time line of Steve Jobs's life:

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco
  • 1972 - Enrolled at Reed College
  • 1976 - Founded Apple Computer Company
  • 1985 - Resigned at Apple and founded NeXT Inc
  • 1986 - Funded The Graphics Group (later renamed Pixar)
  • 1997 - Returned to Apple
  • 2007 - Introduced the first iPhone
  • 2008 - Introduced the AppStore
  • 2010 - Introduced the first iPad
  • 2011 - Passed away

Stay Hungry. Stay Foolish.

- Steve Jobs
